The Data Structures and Algorithms course equips students with foundational concepts critical for efficient data management and processing. It covers diverse data structures such as arrays, linked lists, and trees, alongside algorithms for searching, sorting, and graph processing. Students learn to analyze algorithm complexity and apply problem-solving techniques in practical scenarios. Through implementing algorithms in Java, the course fosters programming proficiency and debugging skills. Assessments involve both theoretical exams and practical coding tests, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of applying theoretical knowledge to real-world problems, crucial for software development and computational problem solving.
